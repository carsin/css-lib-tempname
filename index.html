Typography

Gripping text for exuberant story-telling.

Veritas CSS uses custom typography rules by default to create gripping and beautiful looking text. All headers, paragraphs, links, hell even underline, strong and emphasized text have meticulously styled down to the finest detail, including line-height, letter spacing, and font weight. If the default settings don't suit your fancy, you can customize them to your liking.
